What is CAGR?

Compound annual growth rate shows the constant yearly rate that would turn the starting value into the ending value over the selected period.

Formula: (Ending value ÷ Starting value)^(1 ÷ Years) − 1

For educational purposes only. Results do not include deposits, withdrawals, taxes, fees, or inflation.
